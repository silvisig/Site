Andrey Boyko

Moscow

Andrey Boyko was born in a small town Saratov in Russia. But now living and teaching at Moscow. Today Andrey - dancer, choreographer and teacher in a modern dance studios in Moscow. He starts to dance when he was 13 and now he one of the famous dancehall dancers and teachers in Russia! more than 15 years of dancing, and more than 10 years of teaching... in 2005 he found himself in Dancehall, and fall in love with this dance. Now he has his own attitude and feelings on this dance, its makes him different!
